THE FACILITIES

Classrooms
The School has 60 classrooms on campus. Every classroom has a public address system, digital clocks, display and writing boards, lockers for students, custom made furniture, and a provision for broadband Internet connection, multimedia support and air conditioning.

PlayGround
The adjacent playground which we share with the American School of Bombay includes a grass pitch, tennis and basketball courts, providing opportunity for a range of outdoor sports, measuring about 2.3 acres. The school campus also has 2 small playground for LKG & UKG classes and for the primary classes. There is also a volleyball court within the school campus.

Laboratories
The laboratories for Physics, Chemistry and Biology and the specialist teaching rooms for Social Science, Languages, Computer Studies, and Mathematics are equipped in accordance with the specifications of national and international examination boards. The science Lab houses 21 Laptops aiding the study of Science and Robotics.

Computer Lab
Computer labs, and Mathematics are equipped in accordance with the specifications of national and international examination boards.The adjacent Lab which we share with the American School of Bombay includes a ipad ,macbook ,glass display providing opportunity for a range of students to take there ongoing project home. The school campus also has 2 small lab for LKG & UKG classes and for the primary classes. There is also a Artificial Intelligence lab within the school campus.

Central Library
The Learning Centre is central to each student's academic progress. In it we have shelf space for some 40,000 books, with already a rich collection. In addition, students have access to periodicals and audio-visual resources, the Internet and photocopying facilities. The Learning Centre is equipped with 18 desktops for reading, reference and research requirement of senior students. The Learning Centre also supports the archiving of the school's various learning resources and records.

Multipurpose Auditorium
The air-conditioned, multipurpose Auditorium on the ground floor provides an elegant, functional and versatile space for assemblies, concerts, lectures and gatherings of all kinds, as well as for a variety of indoor games.The school has a new multi-purpose space at the heart of the school, the Centre for Performing Arts. Fitted with a state-of the art sound system and over 30 theatre lights, the room has become the focal point for performing arts within the school. The school auditorium is equipped with AV facility with high-end BARCO projectors.
